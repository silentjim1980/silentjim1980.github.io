How to find YouTube video URL?

-For YouTube app
  1. Press the share button at the bottom of the YOUTUBE video.
  2. Click "Link Copy" in the popup menu at the bottom.
  3. Paste to the "STEP 1. YouTube Video URL" of site.
  4. Click the "Get Video" to proceed to the next step.
-For Web Browser
  1. Move to the YOUTUBE Video.
  2. Copy the URL in the top of Browser.(Ctrl + C)
  3. Paste to the "STEP 1. YouTube Video URL" of our site.
  4. Click to "Get Video" Button and do Next Step

How to use?


Follow the steps below.
STEP 1
  • After entering the correct YouTube video address, click the "Add Video" button.
  • Add as many as you want.
  • Set the start time of each video in seconds.
STEP 2
  • Click the "Create Multi View" button.
  • Click the Copy icon or View in a new window icon.
